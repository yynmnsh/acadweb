    Current Research Projects

    Ethical AI in Healthcare Decision Making

    NSF CAREER Award | 2024-2029 | $500,000

    This project aims to develop a comprehensive framework for ethical AI deployment in healthcare settings. We are creating tools and methodologies that help healthcare providers understand and trust AI recommendations while ensuring patient safety and equity.

    Key Objectives:

    • Develop interpretable ML models for medical diagnosis
    • Create bias detection tools for healthcare AI systems
    • Design user interfaces that promote appropriate trust in AI
    • Establish evaluation metrics for ethical AI in healthcare

    Personalized Learning with Responsible AI

    NIH R01 Grant | 2023-2026 | $750,000

    In collaboration with the School of Education, this project explores how AI can be used to create personalized learning experiences while protecting student privacy and promoting educational equity.

    Research Questions:

    • How can AI adapt to different learning styles without reinforcing biases?
    • What privacy-preserving techniques work best in educational settings?
    • How do we measure and ensure fairness in educational AI systems?

    Research Areas

    Artificial Intelligence Ethics

    My work in AI ethics focuses on developing practical frameworks and tools that help researchers and practitioners build more ethical AI systems. This includes:

    • Bias Detection: Automated methods for identifying bias in datasets and models
    • Fairness Metrics: New evaluation criteria for measuring fairness across different populations
    • Ethical Guidelines: Practical guidelines for ethical AI development and deployment
    • Stakeholder Engagement: Methods for involving affected communities in AI development

    Interpretable Machine Learning

    Interpretability is crucial for building trust in AI systems, especially in high-stakes applications. My research in this area includes:

    • Model-Agnostic Methods: Explanation techniques that work with any ML model
    • Inherently Interpretable Models: ML models that are interpretable by design
    • Human-Centered Explanations: Explanations tailored to different user needs and expertise levels
    • Evaluation of Explanations: Methods for measuring the quality of ML explanations

    Healthcare AI

    Healthcare presents unique challenges and opportunities for AI. My research addresses:

    • Clinical Decision Support: AI tools that assist healthcare providers in diagnosis and treatment
    • Medical Image Analysis: Deep learning for radiology and pathology applications
    • Health Equity: Ensuring AI systems work fairly across diverse patient populations
    • Privacy Protection: Techniques for protecting patient privacy in AI systems

    Educational Technology

    AI has tremendous potential to transform education. My work focuses on:

    • Personalized Learning: AI systems that adapt to individual learning styles and needs
    • Intelligent Tutoring: AI tutors that provide personalized feedback and support
    • Learning Analytics: Using data to understand and improve learning processes
    • Educational Equity: Ensuring AI-powered education benefits all students equally
